EMPNEO 3.0 is a state level two-day camp aimed at instilling a passion for science and technological innovation in school students. The event will be held on 13th and 14th of July 2019 at Model Engineering College, Thrikkakara. The event is open to School students from classes 9th to 12th. The camp seeks an active participation from the students as the two day event promises to indulge them in various sessions such as Talks by eminent and distinguished speakers, Hands-on workshop sessions, Informative and entertaining Industrial Visits as well as fun and interactive icebreaker sessions. Empneo 3.0 hence seeks to tap the potential of students by 'Illuminating Possibilities'!


ALCHEMY QUIZ

Attention young Quizzers! Alchemy is the exciting Science Quiz conducted as part of Empneo 3.0. The quiz will be held at Model Engineering College on 12th July. The general topic for the quiz would be Science and Technology. The Quiz would consist of 2 rounds: a preliminary round and the final round. Exciting prizes await the winners!
